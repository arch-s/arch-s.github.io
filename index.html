Who Am I

I am a an engineering student who loves to explore the world and make small differences wherever I go.

Given the oppurtunity, I would pull something apart and analyse it to understand what makes it tick. This natural curiosity led me to study engineering to not just pull somthing apart, but create something better. In my time studying electrical and electronic engineering, I found my strengths in signal processing and digital electronics with some ability in software.

In my free time I play the piano, go rock climbing, play badminton and help in backstage theatre.

Wi-Fi Buggy

October '16 - June '17

A project from my first year of university. In a group of six, we designed and created a buggy able to to be driven from a Wi-Fi connected device with the capability of identifying certain minerals. These minerals were identified by their radio, ultrasonic, infra-red and magnetic signature.

Climbing Logger

October '17 - March '18

A system designed to improve the experience of climbers in an indoor climbing centre. It logs the routes a climber has done, the number of attempts, the time taken to do it and their best time. This is stored in a database from which a user can access information about any route and track their progress via a website.
A full report can be found here.

Wristband
